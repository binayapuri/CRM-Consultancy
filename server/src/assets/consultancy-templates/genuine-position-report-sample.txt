Genuine Position Report Sample

Purpose:
Explain why the nominated role is genuine and required for the business.

Suggested sections:
1. Business background and operations
2. Growth in business activity and staffing needs
3. Why the role is required now
4. Reporting line and organizational chart summary
5. Why local recruitment has not filled the role
6. Salary benchmark / AMSR commentary
7. Duties and responsibilities of the position

Attach supporting evidence where available:
- Financial statements
- BAS
- Payroll summaries
- Org chart
- Ads and interview outcomes
